Data Protection

Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Allyoucanreserve Ltd (trading as “bridge.”), registered in England & Wales, Company No. 10162791. Registered office: 124 City Road, London, England, EC1V 2NX.

Email: hello@ai-bridge.restaurant

2. What Data We Collect

We keep data collection to a minimum. Here is what we may collect:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our mailing list via Kit (ConvertKit)
  • Payment information — if you purchase a guide, payments are processed by Stripe via Kit Commerce. We do not store your card details
  • Basic analytics — Cloudflare provides anonymised traffic data (page views, country, browser type). No cookies are set by us for tracking purposes

3. How We Use Your Data

If you provide your email address, we use it solely to send you updates about new guides and resources. We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

If you purchase a guide, your email and transaction data are processed by Kit and Stripe to deliver your purchase and provide customer support if needed.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — hosting and CDN. Cloudflare may collect anonymised analytics data. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Kit (ConvertKit) — email list management and digital product delivery. Kit Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing (via Kit Commerce). Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — font delivery. Google may log anonymised usage data. Google Privacy Policy

5. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Cloudflare may set essential security cookies (e.g. __cflb, __cf_bm) required for the functioning and protection of the website. These are strictly necessary and do not track you for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent — unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time using the link in any email

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@ai-bridge.restaurant. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our mailing list. You can unsubscribe at any time. Purchase records are retained as required by UK tax law.

8. Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS via Cloudflare. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We do not store any financial data on our servers.

9. Children

This website and our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact us at hello@ai-bridge.restaurant.
