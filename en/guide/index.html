How To Get Started

How to gain your free AI assistant in 5 minutes.

This is not theory. Let's set you up right now.

Step 1
Pick your tool

You have two main options. Both are free. Both work on your phone and computer.

ChatGPT (chat.openai.com) - The most popular AI tool in the world. Create a free account in 30 seconds. You can also create a custom assistant that remembers your restaurant's details every time you chat.

Claude (claude.ai) - Made by Anthropic. Excellent for longer tasks like analysing documents, writing menus, or working through complex problems. Also free.

Pick either one. Seriously - you can't go wrong. Create an account with your email and you'll see a text box. That's the whole interface.

Step 2
Make it YOUR assistant

This is the part nobody tells you. Don't just ask random questions. Start by telling AI about your business. This is what turns a generic tool into your personal assistant.

Example
"I manage a 60-cover Italian restaurant in Birmingham. We have 12 staff. Our average spend per head is 28 pounds. We are open Tuesday to Sunday. I am going to be using you regularly to help me with admin, staff management, marketing and daily operations."

Now it's not a generic chatbot. It's an assistant who understands your restaurant, your team and your challenges.

Step 3
Give it a real task

Not a test. Not "what's the capital of France." A real task from today's to-do list. Something you have been putting off. Something that has been sitting in the back of your mind all week.

That job ad. That Google review. That email to your landlord. That training checklist for the new starter. Pick one. Type it. Watch what happens.

A Quick Word

Once you start using AI regularly, you will notice the free models have limits - slower responses, shorter answers, less nuance. The paid versions (around 20 pounds a month for either ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro) are a massive step up. The difference in quality is genuinely night and day.

Think about it this way: 20 pounds a month for an assistant that saves you hours every single day. That's less than a round of drinks. Less than a single cover on a Friday night. For something that can genuinely transform how you run your business.

But don't rush into it. Play with the free tier first. Get comfortable. Try the tasks in this guide. If you find yourself thinking "this is actually incredible" - and you will - then upgrade.

You can always cancel and go back to free. Both platforms make it easy. No contracts, no hassle.

If the free tier makes you think "wow, this is so helpful" - the paid models will blow you away.

Being Honest

What AI can and can't do.

AI is powerful. But it's not magic. Being honest about its limits will actually make you better at using it.

AI is brilliant at:

- Writing, rewriting and improving any text - emails, menus, job ads, social posts, review replies

- Explaining complex things in plain language

- Brainstorming and generating creative ideas on demand

- Analysing data you give it - costs, sales figures, rotas, timesheets

- Translating menus and communications naturally

- Creating checklists, SOPs and training materials from scratch

- Troubleshooting equipment problems

- Giving you a second opinion on any decision

Always double-check:

- Specific numbers, legal requirements and regulations

- Very recent events or changes - AI may not have today's news

- Hyper-local details about your specific area

- Anything involving your brand's unique voice - use AI as a starting point, then add your personality

AI will never replace:

- The warmth of your smile when a guest walks in

- The instinct you have built over years of reading a room

- The taste of a dish made by human hands

- The relationships you build with your regulars

- You. AI is your assistant. Not your replacement.

The golden rule: Use AI for the work you have to do so you have more time for the work you love to do. Let it handle the admin, the emails, the writing. You handle the people, the food, the magic.
