A Taste[SM.5]

This is what
one prompt
looks like.

You don’t need to learn prompt engineering. You just copy, paste, and let AI do the work.

Example Prompt

You are my restaurant’s social media manager. My restaurant is [name], a [concept] in [city]. Our style is [casual/upscale/etc]. Our audience is [describe]. Write me 5 Instagram post captions for this week. Include: one about our [signature dish], one behind-the-scenes kitchen moment, one staff spotlight, one asking followers a question, and one promoting our [upcoming event/special]. Keep the tone [warm/playful/professional]. Add 10 relevant hashtags to each.

One prompt. Five posts. Two minutes. The guide includes dozens of these — covering every content type across every platform.
